## 🔧 What Was Done

### Issue Resolution
**Problem:** RenderFlex overflow warnings on song cards  
**Solution:** Added `mainAxisSize: MainAxisSize.min` to Column widget  
**Result:** Clean, error-free rendering ✅

#### 1️⃣ Enhanced Search Bar (🔍)
- Live search suggestions (up to 8 results)
- Album art preview
- One-tap playback
- Smooth focus animations
- Clear button for quick reset
